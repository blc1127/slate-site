Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. What Slate processes

Slate may process the following data to provide moderation features:

  • Discord user IDs and guild (server) IDs
  • Moderation actions (warn/strike/timeout/kick/ban) and reasons
  • Configuration settings for your server (roles, toggles, thresholds)

2. What Slate does not store

  • Passwords
  • Payment information
  • Direct messages content (unless you explicitly build features that do)

3. Why we use this data

  • To enforce server-configured moderation rules
  • To keep an audit trail of actions (infractions)
  • To protect servers from spam and raids

4. Data retention

Data is retained as needed for moderation history and server configuration. Server owners may request deletion.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data. Data may be processed by hosting providers as required to operate the service.

7. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy. Updates will be posted on this page.
